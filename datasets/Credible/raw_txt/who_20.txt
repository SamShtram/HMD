IOAC Membership
The Committee consists of up to 12 members drawn from national governments, NGOs, the UN system, donors, the private sector, academia, and civil society, with extensive experience in a broad range of disciplines.
Members serve in their personal capacity and exercise their responsibilities with full regard for the paramount importance of independence.
