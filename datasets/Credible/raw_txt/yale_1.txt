Patient Tools
We want everything about your experience with Yale Medicine to be as easy and convenient as possible. The Patient Tools section of our website is designed to provide you with the information and resources you need, from scheduling an appointment, to preparing for both in-person and telehealth visits, to getting a second opinion from a Yale Medicine specialist.
If you have questions that aren't answered on our website, please contact our Yale Medicine Coordination, Appointment, Referral & Engagement (CARE) Center at 1-877-YALEMDS (877-925-3637).
