The triple billion targets provide a unified approach to
accelerating progress towards the achievement of the health-related
Sustainable Development Goals.
Estimates are calculated with 90% uncertainty intervals (UI) and
presented as the upper and lower bounds in the closed brackets after the
mean estimate.
